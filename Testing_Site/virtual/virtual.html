Virtual Student Union Discord:

  1. As a result of current events surrounding COVID-19, the Felician Community has created a Discord where students can congregate and keep up with current events, aside from notifications we recieve through email.
  1. You can use the Discord to ask for help from colleagues, advice on projects from the community; post questions you might have; etc. It is a gathering space for all Computer Science & Cybersecurity majors, faculty, staff, even administrators! I would ask that you join our Discord server.
  1. Apologies that it cannot be a direct sign-up, but because of Federal Law, we have to ensure that those who belong are members of our community. We hope to see you there!
