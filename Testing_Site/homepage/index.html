About Us

This is a place where Felician University students can obtain some online resources, such as videos or information sites, to assist them in their computer science and cyber-security needs while we continue our journey as a virtual learning space campus.

Online resources are convenient and accessible. Student may favor different resources depending on their interest or learning style; however, the internet tends to be diverse enough to fit everyone's needs one way of another.
